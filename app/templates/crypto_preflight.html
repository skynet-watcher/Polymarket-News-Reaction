How to read this page

  • ✅ Ready — Parser understood the market rule, Binance confirms the opening price, and both YES/NO books have enough liquidity. This market is ready to trade against.
  • ⏳ Future candle — The candle hasn't started yet so Binance has no data. It will become tradeable once the candle opens.
  • 📭 No orderbook — Parsed correctly and Binance is fine, but the Polymarket orderbook for YES or NO doesn't have enough liquidity right now.
  • 🔍 Needs review — The parser couldn't confidently extract the candle interval or asset. These markets need a human to check the rule text.
  • ⛔ Unsupported — Not an Up/Down candle market (e.g. price threshold, all-time high). Not tradeable with this strategy.
